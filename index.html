ShopSense AI

Shopsense AI is an innovative browser extension designed to enhance the Amazon shopping experience. Leveraging the power of artificial intelligence, specifically generative AI and natural language processing, this extension revolutionises the way buyers analyse products based on customer reviews. With Shopsense AI, users gain valuable insights into product quality, features, and overall customer satisfaction by utilising Amazon reviews as a primary data source. The extension utilises advanced algorithms to extract and summarise relevant information from thousands of reviews, saving buyers valuable time and effort in conducting their own research.
